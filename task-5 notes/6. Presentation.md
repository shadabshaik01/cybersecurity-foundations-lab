# Presentation

## Objective
To effectively communicate the results of the capstone project through a structured video walkthrough and a clear, well-organized GitHub repository.

## Theory & Concepts
- **Communication Skills**: The ability to explain complex technical issues to a non-technical audience.
- **Structure of a Pitch**:
  - The Problem (Vulnerabilities found).
  - The Impact (What could go wrong).
  - The Solution (How we fixed it/mitigated it).
- **GitHub Best Practices**: Clear README, organized folders, and well-commented code/notes.

## Tools Used
- OBS Studio / Zoom (for recording)
- PowerPoint / Google Slides

## Commands & Practical Steps
- **Presentation Outline**:
  - Slides 1-2: Intro & Project Goals.
  - Slides 3-5: Key findings with screenshots.
  - Video Clip: 2-3 min live demo of a critical exploit.
  - Slides 6-7: Mitigation strategies and final thoughts.

## Practical Demonstration Summary
Created a 12-minute walkthrough video of the capstone project. Demonstrated key exploits and explained the corresponding defensive measures. Verified that all repository links and documents are accessible for the final review.
